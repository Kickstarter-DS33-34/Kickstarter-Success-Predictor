About Kickstarter

Kickstarter is a new model for bringing creative projects to life. With fewer barries to entry than traditional funding models, Kickstarter offers a great chance to make your idea a reality.

How Our Predictor Works

We looked at over 200,000 Kickstarter campaigns to see what works and what doesn't. You tell us a little bit about your kickstarter campaign (location, length, etc.) and we tell you your chances of meeting your financial goal.

Want to Optimize Your Campain?

Our predictor allows you to see what features may increase or decrease your chances of meeting your goals. For example, maybe increasing your campaign length from 1 month to 2 months boosts your chances of success by 20%
